The New York Rundown is the go-to resource for the city's running community. We bring you the best information to keep you on the move in the city that never sleeps.

The New York Rundown will offer:

  • A calendar of all the running-related events in town;
  • The latest news of interest to NYC runners;
  • Reports on races and routes;
  • Guides to teams, shopping, and running-related services;

And much more coming soon.
